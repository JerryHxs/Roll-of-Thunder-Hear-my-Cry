Mildred D. Taylor
Mildred DeLois Taylor is an African-American writer known for her works exploring the struggle faced by African-American families in the Deep South.

Caroline Logan
Caroline Logan, or referred as Big Ma in the book Roll of Thunder Hear My Cry, is a strong independent widow and mother of her 2 sons: David and Hammer.
Mary Logan
Mary Logan, or referred as Mama in the book Roll of Thunder Hear My Cry, is the mother of the main character of the book and 3 other children and is the wife of David.
